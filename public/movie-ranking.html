The Best Movies According to Magid

My top 3 movies of all time.

Tetris (2022)

A fun film showcasing the nerve-wracking beginning to an otherwise innocent video game.

Free Guy (2021)

A comedy showcasing about a popular video game character who grapples with his reality not being the true reality.

Spiderman:Across the Spiderverse (2023)

A visually stunning animated film about a superhero trying to balance his two lives.
